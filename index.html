About me

AI researcher and lecturer with 10+ years of experience in Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Deep Learning. Ph.D. in Computer Science (University of Tours, France, 2020). Head of Computer Science Division, Hong Duc University. Published more than 20 international papers (Elsevier, Springer, IEEE, Scopus/WoS) in medical imaging, NLP, and AI applications. Supervised PhD candidates and Master students in AI-related topics. Research interests include computer vision, deep learning, natural language processing, and AI for healthcare, agriculture, and education.

Motivation

I am passionate about leveraging AI to solve real-world problems and improve people's lives. My motivation is to contribute to the advancement of AI research and its applications in various domains, particularly in healthcare, agriculture, and education. I am driven by the desire to create innovative solutions that can make a positive impact on society and help address some of the most pressing challenges we face today.
